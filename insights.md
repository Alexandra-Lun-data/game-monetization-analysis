# Insights

## 1. Paying Users Share
The overall share of paying users is **18%** of all registered players.  
Character race has only a minor effect on this metric:
- Demon — 19%
- Angel — 17%
- Elf — 17%

This suggests that race is not a strong driver of payment behavior.

## 2. Purchase Statistics
A total of **1,307,678** purchases were made for **686,615,040** premium currency units.

Main characteristics:
- Minimum purchase amount: **0**
- Maximum purchase amount: **486,615.1**
- Mean purchase amount: **525.69**
- Median purchase amount: **74.86**
- Standard deviation: **2517.34**

The large gap between mean and median indicates a strongly skewed distribution.

## 3. Anomalous Purchases
Zero-value purchases account for only **0.07%** of all purchases (**907 transactions**), which means anomalies are rare.

## 4. Most Popular Epic Items
Top-selling epic items:
1. **Book of Legends**
2. **Bag of Holding**
3. **Necklace of Wisdom**

These items account for the majority of purchases, which suggests high concentration of player demand.

## 5. Race Activity Analysis
The share of buyers and paying players is similar across races, but some races differ in purchase frequency and average purchase amount.

- Human and Angel players make more purchases per user.
- Northman and Elf players have the highest average purchase value.

This may indicate different monetization patterns across player segments.
